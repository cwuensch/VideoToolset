Info (clip)

Present in v2.5. It gives info of a clip printed in the left corner of the clip. The info consists of the colorspace, size, fps, whether it is field (you applied SeparateFields) or frame based (you didn't apply SeparateFields), whether AviSynth thinks it is bottom (the default in case of AviSource) or top field first, whether there is audio present, the number of channels, sample type and the samplerate. In v2.55 a CPU flag is added with supported optimizations.

Example:

AviSource("C:\filename.avi").Info

Results in a video with information in the top left corner: 

Frame: 12 
ColorSpace: YV12 
Width: 720 pixels, Height: 432 pixels 
Frames per second: 25.0000 
Fieldbased (Separated) Video: NO 
Parity: Assuming Bottom Field First 
Video Pitch: 720 bytes. 
Has Audio: yes 
Audio Channels: 6 
Sample Type: integer 16 bit 
Samples Per Second: 44100
CPU detected: x87 MMX ISSE SSE 3DNOW 3DNOW_EXT
