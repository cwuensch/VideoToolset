SangNom beta - "readme"

Then, you need:
- YV12-interlaced
- Processor with iSSE (Athlon TBird+, PIII+) 

Syntax:

SangNom (order, aa)

Order:
0 -> use only top field
1 -> use only bottom field
2 -> alternates, to make the bob: DoubleWeave.SangNom(order=2)

Aa: (antialiasing)

To avoid that face anything algo produces too complex and sharp edges
(To make result kanji-proof)

Default call of SangNom() is equivalent to: SangNom(order=1, aa=48)
